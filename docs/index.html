Unit Converter

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Unit Converter is a free Android app that converts between units of length, weight, temperature, area, volume, and speed. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information directly. The app does not require account creation or login.

However, the app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. advertising ID)
  • IP address and general location (country/region)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • Device type and operating system version

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

How the Information Is Used

AdMob uses collected data solely to serve relevant advertisements. We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share any user data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates Google AdMob, which is subject to Google's own privacy practices. We are not responsible for the data practices of Google or any other third-party service.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data ourselves, there is no personal data stored on our servers. All ad-related data is handled securely by Google's infrastructure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
